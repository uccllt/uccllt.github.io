Grammar Exercise, Habitual, Progressive, Simple Future Tense Forms (Lesson 18)

Quiz

 

  1. Me da dabiara.

    1.   I am sleeping.
    2.   I sleep everyday.
    3.   I slept everyday.
    4.   I will sleep everyday.

  2. Kofi di kwadu.

    1.   Kofi is eating banana.
    2.   Kofi will eat banana.
    3.   Kofi ate a banana.
    4.   Kofi eats banana.

  3. Me rekɔ sukuu.

    1.   I am going to school.
    2.   I will go to school.
    3.   I went to school.
    4.   I have been to school.

  4. Ɔkɔ asɔre Kwasiada biara.

    1.   He went to church every Sunday.
    2.   He is going to church this Sunday.
    3.   He goes to church every Sunday.
    4.   He will go to church every Sunday.

  5. Yɛ rekɔ sra no.

    1.   We will visit him.
    2.   We visit him..
    3.   We visited him.
    4.   We are going to visit him.

  6. Ɔredi aduane no.

    1.   He is about to eat the food.
    2.   He will eat the food.
    3.   He is eating the food.
    4.   He is going to eat the food.

  7. Yɛ da mpa so.

    1.   We are sleeping on a bed.
    2.   We are going to sleep on a bed.
    3.   We slept on a bed.
    4.   We sleep on a bed.

  8. Afua kɔ adwuma.

    1.   Afua goes to work.
    2.   Afua is going to work.
    3.   Afua went to work.
    4.   Afua will go to work.

  9. Yɛ rekɔ hwɛ TV.

    1.   We are watching TV.
    2.   We are going to watch TV.
    3.   We will watch TV.
    4.   We watched TV.

  10. Mɛ kɔ sukuu.

    1.   I am going to school.
    2.   I went to school.
    3.   I will go to school.
    4.   I am about to go to school.

  11. Kofi bɛda mpa no so.

    1.   Kofi will sleep on the bed.
    2.   Kofi is sleeping on the bed.
    3.   Kofi slept on the bed.
    4.   Kofi is about to sleep on the bed.

  12. Me reda.

    1.   I will sleep.
    2.   I slept.
    3.   I am sleeping.
    4.   I will be sleeping.

  13. Wo da ntɛm.

    1.   You sleep early.
    2.   You are sleeping early.
    3.   You slept early.
    4.   You will sleep early.

  14. Mɛ hu no.

    1.   I saw him.
    2.   I am about to see him.
    3.   I can see him.
    4.   I will see him.

  15. Wo bɛdi.

    1.   You will eat it.
    2.   You are eating it.
    3.   You ate it.
    4.   You can eat it.
