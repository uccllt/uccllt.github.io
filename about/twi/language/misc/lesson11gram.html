Lesson11 - Grammer

Quiz

 

  1. Ɛkɔm de me.

    1.   He is hungry.
    2.   I am sad.
    3.   I am thirsty.
    4.   I am hungry.

  2. Me ani kum.

    1.   I am sleepy.
    2.   I am dead.
    3.   I will kill.
    4.   My eye hurts

  3. Sukɔm de me.

    1.   I am sleepy
    2.   I am hungry
    3.   I am thirsty
    4.   She is hungy.

  4. Awɔ de me.

    1.   I am sleepy.
    2.   I am thirsty.
    3.   I am hungry.
    4.   I am cold.

  5. Ahuhuro de no.

    1.   He is feeling hot.
    2.   I am feeling hot.
    3.   I am thirsty.
    4.   I am hungry.

  6. Dwonsɔ de me.

    1.   I want to walk.
    2.   I want to "pee"
    3.   I want to sing.
    4.   I feel like running.

  7. Ɛkɔm de wo anaa?

    1.   Is he hungry?
    2.   Are you thirsty?
    3.   Are you hungry?
    4.   Are we hungry?

  8. Awɔ bɛ de wo.

    1.   You will be cold.
    2.   You will be hot.
    3.   He will be hot.
    4.   I will be cold.

  9. Awɔ wo mu.

    1.   I will be hot.
    2.   It is cold.
    3.   It is hot.
    4.   It is inside.

  10. Ahuhuro wo mu.

    1.   Its going to be hot
    2.   It is cold.
    3.   It is hot.
    4.   Its going to be cold.
