The Twi on-the-go Project is an online cooperative project between the Department of Linguistics at the University of California at Los Angeles and the Center for African Studies at the University of California at Berkeley. The goal of the Twi on-the-go Project is not to develop a whole suite of stand-alone teaching materials for Twi, but just an introduction to the language, a series of illustrative pre-lessons, that will get students jazzed about learning an African language, and will supplement their classes here and in Ghana.
